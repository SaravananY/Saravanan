Resume

Nowadays I am a Ph.D. student at KAUST. I am working on nonlinear Hamilton-Jacobi PDEs and mean-field games with applications to crowd motion dynamics. My research interests include general problems of existence of solutions and numerical methods for mean-field games, viscosity solutions, and deterministic and stochastic optimal control problems with applications to quantitative finance and economics.
